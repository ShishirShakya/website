# Research software and web platforms

- **MyMeritGuide** (Founding Engineer): [mymeritguide.com](https://mymeritguide.com/). Agentic-AI platform for scalable, FERPA-aligned oral assessment and authentic learning validation. Full-stack delivery on AWS (React/TypeScript/Vite, containerized API on ECS Fargate, PostgreSQL/RDS, S3, CloudFront).
- **Domain-Agnostic Meta-Analysis Workspace**: Schema-driven systematic review and quantitative meta-analysis pipeline (structured extraction CSVs, PRISMA-oriented wiki synthesis, validation tooling) for reproducible evidence synthesis across applied research domains.
- **Court Opinions Research Corpus**: Automated harvest and indexing of U.S. judicial opinions from the CourtListener API into structured cluster/opinion bundles for legal/economic analysis.
- **Historical News Archive Pipeline**: Playwright-based automation for systematic capture and cataloging of newspaper coverage from licensed digital archives for qualitative and historical policy research.
- **Academic Literature Knowledge Base**: Gradio web interface for citation-grounded Q&A over an LLM-maintained research wiki (wikilinks, BibTeX export, embedded knowledge graph) supporting literature synthesis.
